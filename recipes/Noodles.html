Noodles

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 (16 ounce) container cottage cheese
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

Step 1:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 2:

In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sauté until translucent.

Step 3:

Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and add to the pot with the onions.

Step 4:

In a large bowl, combine the cottage cheese, sour cream, sea salt, and black pepper. Mix well.

Step 5:

Add the cottage cheese mixture to the pot with the noodles and onions. Stir until well combined.

Step 6:

Transfer the noodle mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Spread evenly.

Step 7:

Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top.

Step 8:

Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Enjoy your homemade noodles!
